A general-purpose framework for screening text records by topic
relevance: multilingual embedding cosine scoring, zero-shot
classification, a lexical keyword layer, and a learned selection model
over human keep/drop labels. Built for the planscanR family
(environmental-assessment records) but usable with any tibble of text
and a topic vector. Brings in the Python toolchain (via reticulate) that
the leaf planscanR fetcher deliberately avoids.
